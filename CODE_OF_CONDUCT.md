# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a respectful, harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that helps:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and other
conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer via a
[GitHub security advisory](https://github.com/aneebbaig/acca-to-job-ready/security/advisories/new)
or a direct message. The maintainer will review and respond, and may remove
comments, commits, code, issues, or contributors that violate this policy.
